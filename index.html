Services

We offer a number of services.

Quality Used Cars

We have a wide range of quality vehicles in stock, for you to come and view at your convenience.

We do offer a Part Exchange service for your exisiting vehicle at a competitive market evaluation.

Checklist

We do a thorough 100 point checklist to ensure all our vehicles are upto the standard we expect.

MOT & Servicing

All our vehicles are fully MOT and Serviced, before leaving our dealership. We will provide all paperwork to put your mind at ease.

We can provide the VC5 and Insurance service to make sure you are ready to hit the road running ...


We can provide a Click and Collect service and accept MOT Failure (Categories: A-S).

We also accept car registration plates from 2010 (60) until 2021 (71).

About

More About Kenkirk Auto's ...

  • 2018 - 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started with a idea of providing quality used cars and expanding our exisiting car stock with registrations years: 2010 upto 2021.

  • 2019 - 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    With our expanding used car stock, we were looking on how to expand the business, we decided on implementing a click and collect system to make it easier for the consumer, start the process of selecting a used car from the great range of stock we provide.

  • 2020 - Present

    Where We Are Today ...

    We are bringing you quality used cars as well providing great servicing and mot services with our 100 point checklist to ensure our cars meet the standard before leaving our forecourt.

    We will be expanding with our services and quality used car stock, so keep checking in with us.
